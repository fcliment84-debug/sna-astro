Our journey

Over twenty-five years building a different kind of acoustic engineering: from field knowledge to proprietary technology.

Ten milestones that define who we are.

01 Pre-2014

Where it all begins: field knowledge

Before SNA existed as a company, its technical team had accumulated over 15 years of direct experience in industrial acoustic engineering, energy projects, critical infrastructure, and advanced vibration studies. This expertise is not learned from a book — it is forged by solving real problems in complex environments.

02 2014

An acoustic engineering firm with a different calling is born

SNA is founded with a clear premise: to apply real technical rigour to acoustic engineering, far from generalist approaches. From day one, the company focuses on delivering field solutions that work, not theoretical reports that end up in a drawer.

03 2016

Pioneers in acoustic diagnostic technology

SNA imports and integrates the first acoustic camera in Spain — a diagnostic tool that allows visualising sound behaviour in real time. This milestone is a turning point: SNA goes from being a competent firm to becoming a national technical reference.

04 2017

Positioning in the most demanding sectors

Thanks to the acquired technical capability, SNA positions itself in sectors where acoustics cannot afford errors: energy industry, data centres, and critical infrastructure. The company leaves behind generalist consultancy and establishes itself as a high-level acoustic engineering firm.

05 2018–2020

When conventional solutions are not enough

SNA identifies that conventional acoustic insulation solutions have structural limitations the market accepts as inevitable. Instead of accepting them, it launches a proprietary R&D line focused on three specific challenges: creating demountable systems, developing ventilated solutions, and achieving effective low-frequency control.

06 2021

An engineered metamaterial that changes the rules of the game

After years of research, SNA conceptualises Basslock®: a modular system with advanced acoustic behaviour, based on geometry control and wave propagation. It is not just another material — it is an engineered metamaterial that solves what traditional insulation cannot.

07 2022

Basslock® opens the door to new markets

With Basslock® technically validated, SNA expands into sectors previously beyond its reach: sports and major events, HVAC machinery, manufacturing, and civil construction.

08 2023–2024

National patent and international PCT extension

SNA formally registers the Basslock® technology in Spain and extends its protection internationally via the PCT treaty. This is not merely a legal formality — it is a strategic decision that safeguards innovation and prepares it for global scaling.

09 2025

From engineering firm to technology company

SNA takes the organisational leap: creates a corporate holding that separates engineering activity from product development, establishes an exclusive licensing model, and designs a structure built for international replicability.

10 2026

Europe, manufacturing, and global alliances

SNA begins its internationalisation phase with three simultaneous axes: entering the European patent phase (EPO), launching industrial manufacturing, and establishing strategic alliances with international partners. The field knowledge that started over 15 years ago is preparing to scale without borders.
