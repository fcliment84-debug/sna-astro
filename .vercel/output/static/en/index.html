Acoustic engineering with independent judgement.

SNA was founded with the conviction that acoustic engineering can — and must — go beyond mere regulatory compliance. Over more than twenty-five years we have developed our own methodology, based on in-depth diagnosis, bespoke design and manufacturing and execution with full technical responsibility.

We are certified as an Innovative SME, with a patented product — Basslock® — developed in response to a specific technical problem that the market could not solve: effective low-frequency sound insulation with airflow.

Our team combines training in acoustic, mechanical and civil engineering with accumulated experience in industrial, building and environmental projects. We do not apply catalogue solutions. We design the answer to each project's specific problem.

Expertise 2002–2017

The technical foundation that makes the difference

SNA was born from real field knowledge. Over a decade of experience in industrial acoustics, the import of the first acoustic camera in Spain and a progressive specialisation in the most demanding sectors — energy, data centres, critical infrastructure — built a technical foundation that is our greatest differentiator today.

Innovation 2018–2024

From applied research to an unprecedented metamaterial

When conventional solutions were no longer enough, we created our own. Years of in-house R&D resulted in Basslock®, an engineered metamaterial that changes the rules of acoustic insulation. A technology protected by national and international patents, already applied in sectors from the energy industry to major events.

Scale 2025–2026

From Spanish engineering to global technology

SNA has evolved from a specialised engineering firm to a technology company with an industrial structure. A corporate holding, a licensing model designed for replicability and a European expansion strategy underway. The field knowledge that started it all is preparing to scale without borders.
