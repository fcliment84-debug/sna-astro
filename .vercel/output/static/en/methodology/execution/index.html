Full technical responsibility, from start to finish.

At SNA, execution is not the delivery of a report. It is the physical application of the designed solution, with the same technical rigour as diagnosis and design, and with the final measurement that verifies the result.

Manufacturing solutions →

In-house manufacturing, site coordination and final verification.

At SNA we directly manufacture and execute the solutions we design. This is not merely a logistical advantage — it is what allows us to guarantee that what is installed corresponds exactly to what was calculated.

Our manufacturing capabilities include industrial acoustic enclosures, machinery enclosures, absorption and insulation panels, acoustic screens and silencers.

In building projects, we coordinate the installation of acoustic systems on site and work with construction teams to ensure that the installation preserves the calculated performance.

Solutions manufactured to measure for each project.

In-house manufacturing is partly possible thanks to the technical development accumulated over years of projects. We do not replicate commercial systems — we manufacture the elements the design requires.

The project doesn't end with installation.

Every project concludes with an on-site measurement that verifies the result against the project's technical objective.

When results fall short of the objective, we identify the cause and correct it. Full technical responsibility also means accepting the necessary adjustments.
